While all divorces involve the same basic issues, a high-net-worth case presents unique concerns that may affect how the legal process unfolds. The key to a successful outcome in these situations is working with a high asset divorce law firm in Manhattan who understands the intricacies of this type of legal matter.

Consulting with a high asset divorce law firm is crucial to avoid improper asset valuation which could lead to unfair distribution and significant financial losses.

Working with a high asset divorce law firm can help ensure that all assets are fairly assessed, preventing disputes over asset value that could negatively impact your financial future.

Why New York High Net Worth Divorces Can Be Complicated

Divorces deal with many complex issues, but a high net worth divorce can present more hurdles than the average divorce. This may involve more complicated considerations about the division of assets, income sources, and debts. The assets that a couple owns can include real estate, investment portfolios, savings and checking accounts, retirement and pension accounts, cash, business interests, and other valuable items. Partnering with a high asset divorce law firm can help navigate these complexities efficiently.

The law considers any property acquired during marriage as marital property, so splitting this property fairly in a divorce is essential. Having a skillful attorney and team of professionals from a high asset divorce law firm can help ensure that the assets are identified, accounted for, and fairly divided.

New York is an equitable distribution state, and this means that a court will divide a couple’s assets equally (though not necessarily right down the middle). However, even if you are in an equitable distribution state, dividing marital property can be extremely complicated in a high net worth divorce. This is because the value of a particular asset can fluctuate over time. As a result, you may need valuation experts from a high asset divorce law firm to help determine how much an asset is worth in order to divide it properly.

It is also common for couples to have separate property assets. These may include assets acquired or businesses started prior to the marriage, as well as inheritance. While separating these assets from the marital property is typically important for both parties, it can be more difficult in a high net worth divorce because of the potential for one spouse to try to hide or undervalue certain assets. A high asset divorce law firm is essential in deploying strategies like employing forensic accountants to uncover hidden assets and ensure that all assets are fairly distributed.

Determining who will keep the home in a divorce can be challenging, especially when children are involved. In a high net worth divorce, the question is usually more complicated because there are more homes to consider, including vacation properties and luxury homes. The decision is often based on whether both partners can afford to maintain the current property. Additionally, consulting a high asset divorce law firm can provide legal guidance on how to minimize the disruption of their child’s life as they go through a difficult separation.

Some high-net-worth couples enter into prenuptial or postnuptial agreements that outline how assets will be split up in the event of a divorce. While New York law allows for these agreements, they must meet strict legal requirements and be executed voluntarily to be valid. Neglecting to review and understand these agreements could prove costly during a divorce, so it is essential to seek the assistance of a skilled attorney to make sure you are protected.

Engaging a high asset divorce law firm is crucial for ensuring that these assets are properly evaluated and distributed in a fair and just manner.
